OUR DESIGN DECISSIONS

Designing and Prototyping the website

Decisions  We decided on a simple layout that would display majority of the content on the home screen so that any visitor would have an easy time navigating the website. When you first scroll down you will see the products we offer and if you click on one of them it will bring you straight to the shop, this is done so the visitor has a clear idea of what we offer without having to go through the whole website. And when you continue scrolling, you will see the about us section which also has a button on the navigation bar so when you press on it, it will bring you to the section on the home page where that info is. This done so that there is no need for an extra page and for when we have first time visitors to the website who wants to know what we are about then all they would have to do is click the button at the top in the navigation bar and it will bring them straight to the about section.

Testing the protoype After the prototyping we started with the testing fase of the project. We asked our test person to complete a specific task which our design team had decided before. Our questions: Q1:Order a sweater from our website. Q2: Find our social media page via the website. Our test person completed the tasks without any efforts, for except one; It was not clear when the user had put something inside of the shopping basket. Finding the social media was easy and no problems were found there. - Our website is efficient, users can quickly order a product. - Our website is engaging, user experience a welcoming feeling they visit our website. After testing our website is no error free. The layout en design of the webpages is simple, recognisable and easy to understand. 

Conclusion After doing the usability testing we came up with some pop-ups that needed to be placed, so it was more clear to the user when they put something in the shopping basket.
